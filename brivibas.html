Brīvības iela

Deviating a little from my previously described route, I could not skip mentioning Brīvības Street in my story – the main street of Riga, which starts at the foot of “Milda” aka the Freedom Monument and stretches to the border of the city of Riga. I have always found it fascinating that it is divided into three parts – Brīvības Boulevard, Brīvības Street and Brīvības Alley (“gatve”). If I choose to ride the trolleybus no.17 instead of the no.23 on my daily trip to the University of Latvia, most of the route is spent on it.

This street is also important to me, because I once spent my weekdays at Brīvības Street no.40 – working in a public relations agency. At that time, I often was running between Brīvības Street and Raiņa Boulevard, while combining work with university studies. Even now, my work is still related to "Brīvībene" – I work for a company whose office is located in Brīvības Alley.
