Ieriķu iela

Because I usually choose the trolleybus no.23 to go to the University of Latvia, then I will continue my story by initially sticking to this particular trolleybus route. After Vaidavas Street, we turn onto Ieriķu Street, which stretches in length from Biķernieku Street to Zemitāni Railway Station. Ieriķu Street serves as a northern border of Purvciems. The main stop on this street is the shopping center "Domina Shopping".

Purvciems and the next part of Riga on the way to the center - Grīziņkalns, are connected by the Zemitānu bridge, where Ieriķu and Aleksandra Čaka streets meet. The bridge is named in honor of Zemitāni station, while Ieriķu Street itself is named like so because the railway line found there stretches from Riga to Ieriķi in Amata region.
