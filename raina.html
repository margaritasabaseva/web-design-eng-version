Raiņa bulvāris

Then, in the end, I have reached the last stage of my journey – Raiņa Boulevard. On the boulevard named after the most famous Latvian poet is located the main building of my Alma Mater, the University of Latvia. It also houses the faculty of the university where I am currently studying – the Faculty of Computer Science.

Raiņa Boulevard is part of the Riga Boulevard Circle. It stretches past the city canal, the Freedom Monument, there are embassies, restaurants, as well as the University of Latvia at the number 19. It should be mentioned that the Institute of Informatics and Mathematics of the University of Latvia, which is important for the Faculty of Computer Science, is located at the number 29 of the boulevard
